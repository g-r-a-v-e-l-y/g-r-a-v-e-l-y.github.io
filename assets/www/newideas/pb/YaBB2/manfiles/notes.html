General Notes:

  • YaBB is distibuted free of charge: do not abuse this by using code in other programs or selling any portions of YaBB. We encourage everyone to donate in some form or another (see yabb.org for more info).
  • Support is free of charge at http://www.yabb.org/cgi-bin/support/YaBB.pl at a "first-come, first-serve basis". Donations (explained on yabb.org) will get you different ammounts of Preference for Support over those who do not donate.
  • View the "whats_new.txt" file for information on upgrades and bug fixes from previous versions of YaBB through YaBB 1 Final. View the "New_in_Y1Gold.txt" to see the bug/security fixes and new features in this YaBB release.
  • This is the FINAL version of YaBB 1 (Gold). Unless major bugs are reported, no further complete releases will be distributed by www.yabb.org until YaBB version 2 is completed. This means that this version is the last version of YaBB 1 that will be supported. NO MORE CODE SUPPORT will be offered for YaBB 1.
  • For any help on installation and customization, please visit us at http://www.yabb.org.
  • If you visit yabb.org's support forum for help or to report bugs, please be aware of what forum you are posting in. The "YaBB News" forum is not for bugs.. they go in the "Bug Reports" forum. Security leaks need to be email to zef@zefnet.com (please do not post them on any forum for obvious reasons).
