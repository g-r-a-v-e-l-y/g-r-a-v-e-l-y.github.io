--- title: Unsung Hero Revolution date: 2007-09-26 06:36:00.00 -8 ---

Some friend’s-of-a-friend have worked very hard in the east coast music scene to put together a social networking and music sales site with a really kick-ass core concept.

If you are in a band and using myspace to promote, you should check it out: http://www.uhrev.com/

The concept seems a little strange.

Sell your tracks there and keep 75% of the sales price. The remaining 25% goes to the site.

If your fans want to sell your tracks, you get 50% of the sales price and the fan gets 25%. The remaining 25% goes to the site.

Fans get to promote their favorite bands and get a cut, knowing the band will still get more than if they sold the same tracks on itunes or amazon.

There is no DRM.
